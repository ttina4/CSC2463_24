About the Game

The game is a speed clicker, where the player must put as many ingredients as they can into the oven. The end will give a statistic of how many items has been put into the oven. Then, the arduino will give feedback on when the player adds an item to the oven with a glowing green light.

All game elements are hand drawn on Procreate and added meticulously to the game.
